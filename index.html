About FeedLink

FeedLink is a community-driven platform dedicated to bridging the gap between food donors and NGOs working to eliminate hunger. We believe that no food should go to waste when communities need it most.

Our mission is to create a sustainable ecosystem where surplus food from restaurants, households, and food businesses reaches those in need, reducing food waste and fighting malnutrition across the country.
